Tenant Rights and Responsibilities

Common Tenant Rights:
1. Right to habitable living conditions
2. Right to privacy
3. Right to request repairs
4. Right to proper notice before landlord entry
5. Right to security deposit return within legal timeframes

Common Tenant Responsibilities:
1. Pay rent on time
2. Keep the property clean and sanitary
3. Notify landlord of necessary repairs
4. Not damage the property beyond normal wear and tear
5. Follow all terms in the lease agreement

Security Deposits:
Most states require landlords to return security deposits within 14-30 days after move-out, along with an itemized list of any deductions.
